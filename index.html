Frequently Asked Questions

When is Series 1?

Phase 1 already launched! Phase 2 and Phase 3 have not been announced yet.

What are compatible wallets?

Only send your ADA via Yoroi/Daedalus/Nami/CCVault wallets. Do not send money from any exchange like Coinbase, Binance, Kucoin etc. Your ADA would be lost.

What is the EE Royalty Bot?

We are creating a custom automatic Royalty dispersing bot that takes the royalties Endangered Empire makes from secondary sales and automatically disperses a percentage to all holders of any Endangered Empire NFT.

What’s the 3D Serum used for and how do you get one?

It will be used to pair with your 2D Angry Orca and it will convert your 2D NFT into a brand new 3D NFT

How much is mint price?

40 ADA

How do I get on the Whitelist?

You must have the @Silverback Role in our Discord. You achieve this by owning 10+ Phase 1 Angry Orca NFT's.

What is the royalty percentage?

10%. 5% goes back to the wildlife organizations, 2.5% to creators and 2.5% to collectors.

Will we be able to keep both 2D and 3D NFT’s, or will it get burned?

You will be able to keep both 2D and 3D NFT’s!
